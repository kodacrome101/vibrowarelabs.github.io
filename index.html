Resonant Architectures for Analog Computation

Developing low-power, high-coherence signal processors through vibrational geometry and mechanical interference.

About

Founded by Asa Bruss in 2025, Vibroware Labs explores a new paradigm of analog computation using tensioned membrane systems. Our mission is to bring theoretical insight into physical hardware that redefines how computation can be structured and experienced.

Technology

Our core innovation is a resonant membrane computing architecture capable of analog signal transformation. A preprint outlining our method is available at:
DOI: 10.20944/preprints202505.1587.v1

We have filed a provisional patent and are currently prototyping devices for biosignal processing, audio transformation, and embedded analog AI applications.
